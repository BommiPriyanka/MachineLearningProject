How to Use the Glaucoma Detection App

  1. Visit the Homepage: Open the Glaucoma Detection app in your browser or on your device.
  2. Login or Sign Up:
    • If you already have an account, click Login and enter your username and password.
    • If you're new, click Sign Up to create a new account by filling in your name, email, and password.
  3. Upload Eye Image to Find Glaucoma: After logging in, go to the Upload Section. Click on the "Upload Image" button to select and upload an eye image from your device. This image will be analyzed by the AI model to detect signs of glaucoma.
  4. Get Results: Once the image is uploaded, the app will automatically analyze it to find glaucoma. You will then see the result displayed clearly on the screen.
  5. View Report (Optional): You may also receive a basic report or confidence score showing the likelihood of glaucoma.
